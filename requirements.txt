# 🧾 Student Life OS — Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Version: v1.0

## Type: Frontend-only Application

## Target Users: Indian College Students

---

# 1. 🧠 PRODUCT OVERVIEW

## 1.1 Vision

Build an **offline-first, all-in-one student productivity system** that combines planning, studying, tracking, and focus tools into a single interface.

Modern student tools like Notion and MyStudyLife succeed because they combine **tasks, notes, schedules, and tracking in one system** ([Graphic Era University][1])

---

## 1.2 Problem Statement

Students in Indian colleges face:

* Fragmented tools (notes, tasks, schedules in different apps)
* Irregular timetables and exam schedules
* Last-minute preparation patterns
* High distraction environments
* Poor tracking of productivity and study consistency

---

## 1.3 Goals

```yaml
primary_goals:
  - unify_student_life_management
  - improve_time_management
  - increase_focus_and_consistency
  - reduce_app_switching

  - offline_first
```
